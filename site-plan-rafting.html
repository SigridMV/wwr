Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to update our clients about our services and activities, as well as to post images of our work and to attract new clients through online forms in order to generate income for the business.

Audience

The type of person we want visiting our website is young-adult, adventurous, energetic and eager to have new experiences, probably between 18 and 40 years old. They receive high income and visit my site mostly through their mobile device. It is important to consider having a color palette related to the river and nature.
